1. Project Vision & Background

1.1. The Barriers Created by Technology

While technology has advanced rapidly, the digital divide between those who benefit from it and those who do not has only deepened.
I realized that technology, which is meant to enrich our lives, can paradoxically become a barrier for some.
My commitment to lowering these barriers stems from the following experiences:

> A Senior at a Welfare Facility

I witnessed a senior citizen struggling with a long-distance bus reservation, stuck in a frustrating loop with a customer service representative. The issue was simply a delayed information update, but for someone unfamiliar with the concept of an 'update,' it was an insurmountable wall.

> A Personal Experience

Last October, I found myself trapped in an endless loop of receipt authentication while trying to log into my email.
Even though I followed the instructions, the screen kept repeating.
It was a tiring experience that stayed with me;
it proved that technological barriers can affect anyone, regardless of age or expertise.
As technology evolves, its misuse inevitably leads to more complex security procedures.
However, these complex layers create a dilemma by alienating certain groups.
I aim to solve this problem by decoupling account functions into hierarchical layers.

1.2. Functional Decentralization of Accounts

The solution proposed in this project is to separate the 'Master Account' from the 'Temporary Account' used for daily interactions.

> Master Account: Maintains high-strength, complex authentication.

It is responsible for creating TemporaryAccounts and controlling their permissions.
Since it is only used for renewing expired accounts or revoking access in case of a hack, it is accessed infrequently.

> Temporary Account: Operates with a limited lifespan and restricted permissions, allowing users to access services via simple, convenient authentication.

By centralizing complex procedures (such as 2FA or CAPTCHA) only during the Temporary Account renewal phase, this system aims to strike a perfect balance between robust security and user convenience.
